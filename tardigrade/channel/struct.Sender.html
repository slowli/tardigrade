Expand description

Sender for a workflow channel provided to the workflow.

A sender is characterized by the type of messages and the codec used to convert messages from / to bytes.

Unlike Receivers, Sender parts of the channel can be cloned. Another difference is ability to control readiness / flushing when sending messages via channel capacity; if the channel reaches its capacity of non-flushed messages, it becomes not ready to accept more messages.
