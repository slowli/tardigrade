pub fn run<Fut: Future>(self, test_future: Fut) -> Fut::Output

Executes the provided future in the context of this runtime. Thus, the future will have access to globals provided by a runtime such as Workflows, or test-specific globals such as Timers.

This method can be used to handle more complex tests than Self::test(), e.g., ones that do not want to spin a specific workflow.
